Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are extremely important to us. This policy explains how the Mental Health AI platform collects, uses and protects user information.

Information We Collect

Our platform collects limited information necessary to perform mental health assessments and improve the accuracy of our AI detection system. This may include survey responses, basic account information and anonymized usage data.

How We Use Your Data

Collected information is used exclusively for mental health analysis, research insights and improving the performance of the AI detection model. Your responses help us understand behavioral patterns related to stress, anxiety and depression.

Data Protection & Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse or disclosure. All sensitive information is stored securely using modern data protection practices.

User Privacy Rights

Users have the right to request access to their data, update personal information or request deletion of their account data at any time.

Research & Ethical Use

The Mental Health AI platform is designed for research and early mental health awareness. All collected data is handled ethically and in compliance with responsible AI and privacy standards.
